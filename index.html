Our story

We built this because we needed it ourselves.

This company did not start as a clever business idea. It started as a problem we were living. We took two very different roads to the same frustration: being the owner, the assistant, the bookkeeper, and the person still answering email at midnight, all in the same body, on the same day.

Ana built her first business from the ground up, then spent years in the corporate world while raising her family as a single mom. Time was the one thing she never had enough of, and too much of it kept disappearing into busywork that did not move anything forward. Getting that time back was not a luxury to her. It was the whole point.

Desmond came up as a creative, writing rhymes and trading bars in battle rap, where you live or die on how fast you think, how well you read the room, and whether you can land the line when the pressure is on. That world teaches you what no classroom can: how to build something from a blank page, how to perform when it actually counts, and how to keep swinging when quitting is the easy move.

That craft is now backed by daily reps as an educator and builder. Desmond actively teaches a Skool community of 60+ people, showing creative professionals how to use AI to build real apps, tools, and systems instead of just watching tutorials. Teaching it every day has sharpened the standard: if the system is not clear enough to explain, sturdy enough to use, and practical enough for a student or business owner to run, it is not done yet.

Turns out that is exactly what building AI systems takes: reading what a business really needs, crafting the thing, teaching the workflow clearly, and refining it until it hits. The stage changed. The craft did not.

So we built the way out, and now we run our own days on it: an AI agent quietly handling the repetitive work, and a daily briefing waiting every morning before we even sit down. The hours that used to vanish now go to our families and the work only we can do. That is not a sales pitch for us. It is how we actually live.

Why we founded this

We are not selling you something we would not use. We built these systems to get our own time back first, and now we set them up for people who are tired of being the software too.
